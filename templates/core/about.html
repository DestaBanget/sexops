About ShopPet

Our Story

ShopPet was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality pet supplies at affordable prices. What started as a small online store has grown into a trusted destination for pet owners across the country.

Our team consists of passionate pet lovers who understand the special bond between pets and their owners. We carefully select each product in our inventory to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, safety, and value.

Our Mission

At ShopPet, our mission is to enhance the lives of pets and their owners by providing:

  • High-quality pet products that promote health and happiness
  • Exceptional customer service and expert advice
  • Competitive prices that make pet care accessible to all
  • A convenient shopping experience with fast and reliable delivery

Our Commitment

We're committed to supporting animal welfare and environmental sustainability. That's why we partner with local animal shelters and rescue organizations, donating a portion of our profits to help animals in need.

We also prioritize eco-friendly products and packaging whenever possible, reducing our environmental footprint while providing the best for your pets.
